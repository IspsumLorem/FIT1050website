A club for and founded by passionate flyers

Tropical Heights was founded by a group of passionate flyers who wanted to share their love of paragliding. Our goal is to make paragliding accessible to those interested and make the learning curves as fun and enjoyable as possible.

Not sure where to fly?

We've got you covered!

FlightLocator™

FlightLocator™ is our in-house software that uses complicated algorithms and weather patterns to determine the best and safest place to fly for the day. Used by newcomers and veterans alike, FlightLocator™ is your best friend when it comes to having a flight.
